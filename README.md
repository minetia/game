This system is an AI-powered automated trading dashboard designed for mobile-optimized crypto trading. It features a high-end "Cyberpunk Secret Lounge" theme with interactive AI avatars and real-time market data.
​Key Features Summary
​AI Auto-Pilot: An automated trading engine that monitors market conditions and executes trades based on pre-set logic.
​Live AI Briefing: Interactive avatars provide real-time status updates and react to your profit/loss milestones.
​Tactical Control Bar: Quickly adjust your target coin, market type (Spot/Futures), leverage, and trade amount.
​Order Confirmation: A safety step that requires a final "Confirm" before any order is executed to prevent accidental trades.
​Screen Wake Lock: Prevents your mobile device from turning off while trading, ensuring a continuous data connection while you are on the move.
​Integrated Wallet: A visual ledger to track your balance, trade history, and real-time PnL (Profit and Loss).
​Priority TOP 5 (Operational Standard)
​Safety First: Use "Safety Mode" to halt new orders during high volatility while maintaining existing positions.
​Connection Stability: The system includes auto-reconnection logic for WebSocket stability during mobile network switching.
​DCA (Ladder Buy): Automatically average down your entry price during market dips using the staged buy-in settings.
​Stop-Loss Enforcement: Hardcoded safety limits to protect your assets from sudden market crashes.
​Wake-Lock Utility: Essential for mobile users to keep the AI engine running in the foreground without interruption.
